Data Deletion Request

At VoxSpace, you have full control over your data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Instant)

You can delete your account directly inside the app:

  1. Open VoxSpace App
  2. Go to Settings > Account
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your choice. This will immediately remove your data.

Option 2: Web Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by emailing us directly. We will process your request within 48 hours.

Email: support@voxspace.app

Please send the email from the address associated with your VoxSpace account.

Note: Deletion is permanent. All your posts, messages, and uploaded files in "My Bag" will be permanently removed from our servers.
