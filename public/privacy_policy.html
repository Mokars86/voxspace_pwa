Privacy Policy for VoxSpace

Last updated: January 23, 2026

VoxSpace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by VoxSpace.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information primarily to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and profile picture.
  • User Content: Messages, posts, comments, and files stored in "My Bag" or shared in Spaces.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version (used for diagnostics and app optimization).
  • Usage Data: Interactions with posts, spaces, and other users.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • To facilitate communication features like Chat, Audio Calls, and Live Spaces.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service.
  • To provide customer support and troubleshoot issues.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • My Bag: Files stored in "My Bag" are private to you unless explicitly shared. We enforce storage limits based on your account type.
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures (SSL, encryption) to protect your data.

4. App Permissions

To provide certain features, VoxSpace may request access to:

  • Camera & Microphone: For video/audio calls and spaces.
  • Storage: To upload photos, videos, or files to your bag or chats.
  • Notifications: To alert you of new messages and space activities.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@voxspace.app
